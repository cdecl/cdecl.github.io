### `brew` 패키지 관리자 
- <https://brew.sh/index_ko>{:target="_blank"}

```sh
# 설치 
$ /bin/bash -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Homebrew/install/HEAD/install.sh)"

$ brew search iterm2
$ brew install iterm2
```
